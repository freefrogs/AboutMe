about me

Who am I professionally?

Do you know this feeling of enthusiasm when you do something that pleases you. You can sit over this for hours, from morning till evening. The need to implement assumptions is more important then the world that surrounds you. This is my attitude towards programming.
Interesting projects are fuel for me. Of course, sometimes I get angry because something is difficult or does not work as it should… But it’s worth the satisfaction I have after solving the problem.
I hope that my need for coding will not leave me in vain old age and I will never grow from the desire to learn new programming languages and frameworks.

Who am I privately?

Privately, I am an optimist. I like creating new things out of wood, glass or resin. I draw, paint, try new styles.
I love eating and cooking, although sometimes I get a sad layer. I would like to learn how to temper chocolate.
In order not to change into a ball, but also for pleasure I do sports, especially riding a bike and swimming.
When I want to take a break I read or go for long walk. My favorite author is Terry Pratchett, but I am also partial to Master & Margarita by M. Bulgakov, Simon Birch by J. Irving, Lamb: The Gospel According to Biff, Christ's Childhood Pal by Ch. Moore, The Dark Tower by S. King and etc.
